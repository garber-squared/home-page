## Interpretation and Definitions

### Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

- **Account** means a unique account created for you to access our Services or parts of our Services.
- **Application** means any software program provided by us that you download and use on any electronic device, including mobile applications available through Google Play Store or other app distribution platforms.
- **Company** (referred to as either "the Company", "we", "us" or "our" in this Policy) refers to Garber Squared LLC.
- **Cookies** are small files that are placed on your computer, mobile device or any other device by a website, containing details of your browsing history on that website among its many uses.
- **Device** means any device that can access our Services such as a computer, cellphone, or digital tablet.
- **Personal Data** is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
- **Service** refers to the Website and any mobile Applications provided by Garber Squared LLC.
- **Service Provider** means any natural or legal person who processes data on behalf of the Company. This includes third-party companies or individuals employed by us to facilitate our Services, provide services on our behalf, or assist us in analyzing how our Services are used.
- **Usage Data** refers to data collected automatically, either generated by the use of our Services or from the service infrastructure itself (for example, the duration of a page visit, app session time, or button clicks).
- **Website** refers to the Garber Squared LLC website, accessible from https://garbersquared.com
- **You** means the individual accessing or using our Services, or the company or other legal entity on behalf of which such individual is accessing or using our Services, as applicable.

## Types of Data We Collect

### Personal Data

While using our Services, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personal data may include, but is not limited to:

- **Contact Information:** Email address, name, phone number
- **Account Information:** Username, password (encrypted), profile information
- **Communication Data:** Messages, inquiries, or feedback you send to us

We collect this data when you:
- Create an account
- Contact us for support
- Subscribe to our newsletter or updates
- Use interactive features of our Services

### Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you use our Services. This may include:

- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type and version, device type, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Information:** Pages visited, time and date of visit, time spent on pages, clickstream data
- **Mobile Device Information:** Mobile device unique ID, mobile operating system, mobile browser type
- **App Performance Data:** Crash reports, performance metrics, error logs

### Location Data

Some of our Applications may request access to location data with your permission:
- **Precise Location:** GPS and other sensor data from your device
- **Approximate Location:** Derived from IP address or network information

We only collect location data when you explicitly grant permission and only use it for the purposes disclosed at the time of collection.

### Tracking Technologies and Cookies

We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Services and store certain information. Technologies we may use include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies:** Small files placed on your device. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some features of our Services.
- **Web Beacons:** Small electronic files in our Services and emails that permit us to count users who have visited pages or opened emails and gather related statistics.
- **Analytics Tools:** We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand how users interact with our Services.

#### Types of Cookies We Use

- **Necessary/Essential Cookies** (Session Cookies): Essential for providing Services and enabling features. These help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use.
- **Functionality Cookies** (Persistent Cookies): Remember your choices (e.g., login details, language preference) to provide a personalized experience.
- **Analytics Cookies** (Persistent Cookies): Help us understand how visitors use our Services to improve functionality and user experience.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy:

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after account closure for legal compliance
- **Usage Data:** Generally retained for shorter periods unless used for security improvements or required by law

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data, including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Regular security assessments and updates

- Secure data storage with reputable providers

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

For Services that may be used by children or that target audiences including children under 13:

- Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
